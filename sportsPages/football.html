ACL Injury Prevention

The most common injuries in Football occur at the neck/head and the lower body. Common injuries for Football include: ACL tears, Hamstring Strains, Concussions, and ankle sprains.

Sets and Reps Breakdown

ACL Tear:


For ACL tears we strongly reccomend to strenthen the muscles and tendons around the knee through deep range of motion
Backwards Sled: Go until you feel a burn in the quadtricep muscles located above knee and work through it, 1-2 Times a week
VMO Squat: Elevate the heels on a surface and perform squats so that the knee can travel over the toes, 2-3 sets of 12-15 Reps progressively increasing the weight as you get more comfortable.

Hamstring Strain:


Romanian Deadlift: Perform deep dynamic stretches before hand, you want as much range of motion as possible, 1-2 Times a week for 3 sets of 8-12

Concussions:


Neck Curls: Lay on your back and perform slow and controlled neck curls, touching the chin to the chest, 1-2 times a week 3 sets of 25 reps
